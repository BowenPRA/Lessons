The 7 Pillars of RLA Reading

For ESL Students (Vietnamese Vocabulary Support)
RLA and the Real World
The Reading section is 95 minutes long. You will encounter more informational texts (75%) than literary passages (25%). This reflects the real world of the workplace and academia. The test assesses your critical thinking: you must analyze, question, and make an inference from the text.
The 7 Question Tasks
  1. Main Idea: The "helicopter question." Look at the big picture (the forest, not the trees).
  2. Development: Follow the author's route from Point A to Point B.
  3. Language Use: Figure out an unfamiliar word's meaning from its context.
  4. Structure: Ask yourself, "Why did the author put this paragraph here?"
  5. Purpose: Ask yourself, "What did the author want to accomplish?"
  6. Evaluation: Judge the author's evidence, reasoning, or assumptions.
  7. Comparison: Look for differences in formatting, era, or opposing viewpoints.

Purpose: Rhetorical Techniques

Rhetorical techniques are tools writers use (like hammers or wrenches) to persuade the reader or get the job done better.
Technique (Kỹ thuật Tu từ) Definition & Example (Định nghĩa & Ví dụ)
Analogy Comparing two things with similar features.
"A jet pump works much like a drinking straw."
Hyperbole Exaggerating a statement to make a point.
"I've tried a million different ways to open this file."
Parallelism Stringing together phrases with similar structures.
"Unmatched protection, unrivalled flexibility, and unsurpassed style."

Evaluation: Logical Fallacies

Evaluation questions ask you to spot flaws in reasoning, known as a logical fallacy.
Fallacy (Ngụy biện) Definition & Example (Định nghĩa & Ví dụ)
Slippery Slope Claiming a minor action will lead to widespread disaster.
"If we stop this one loan, small business in the state will collapse."
Ad Hominem Attacking the opponent's character instead of their argument.
"Don't trust his science theory; he was suspected of plagiarizing years ago."
Straw Man Exaggerating an opponent's argument to easily knock it down.
