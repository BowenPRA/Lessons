How to Solve Algebra Problems

Easy: Simplifying Expressions

Combine like terms by adding their coefficients.

Example: 3x + 5x + 7
Step 1: Add coefficients: 3 + 5 = 8
Answer: 8x + 7

Medium: Solving Linear Equations

Isolate the variable by using inverse operations.

Example: 2x + 5 = 13
Step 1: Subtract 5 from both sides: 2x = 8
Step 2: Divide both sides by 2: x = 4

Hard: Multi-Step Equations

Combine like terms, move variables to one side, then solve.

Example: 4x + 7 = 2x + 15
Step 1: Subtract 2x from both sides: 2x + 7 = 15
Step 2: Subtract 7 from both sides: 2x = 8
Step 3: Divide both sides by 2: x = 4
