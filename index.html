Hi, I'm Bowen.

My name is Spencer Bowen, but I just go by Bowen. My journey in education has been anything but conventional, and that's exactly what drives my passion for teaching.

I was originally an adjunct professor before transitioning into Math Education through a GK12 research program, doing Math Circle work out of SFSU. I dedicated a few years to Math Circle full-time, followed by an outreach role at the American Institute of Mathematics.

Post-COVID, I relocated to Vietnam to teach at a small independent school. Currently, I am studying in the Moreland Credential Program, and this page serves as a portfolio to showcase my teaching artifacts, interactive lessons, and educational games.

🇻🇳 Da Nang, VN
